Why Projections Matter

Every map is a compromise. To represent a curved surface on a flat plane, one must choose which properties to preserve: Area, Shape, Direction, or Distance.

This tool helps GIS professionals and cartographers visualize these compromises using data from the Global Administrative Areas (GADM) database, allowing you to see exactly how your region of interest is transformed across different mathematical models.

Inspired by "World Map Projections" by Stephen Wolfram & Yu-Sung Chang (1998).
Developed for web map professionals and cartography students.
